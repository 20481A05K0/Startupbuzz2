Snitch:   The Clothing Brand That's Putting a Stop to Streetwear Theft

Streetwear is a popular item today, but high demand and high prices. This makes streetwear a target for thieves. The global apparel industry is estimated to be worth $30 billion in 2020, and by 2021 domestic apparel is predicted to exceed $1 billion in stolen clothing. The new solution to street theft A new clothing brand called Snitch aims to stop street theft. Snitch uses NFC tags to track members' clothes. When the Snitch tag is scanned, it shows the item's serial number and owner's name. This makes it harder for thieves to steal Snitch costumes.

Shark Tank Story


Siddharth Dungarwal, the founder of Snitch clothing, appeared on Shark Tank India in season 2. He was seeking an investment of ₹1.5 crore (US$200,000) for a 0.5% stake in his company. Dungarwal started by explaining the problem that Snitch clothing solves: streetwear theft. He told the Sharks that the global value of the streetwear market is estimated to be ₹20,000 crore (US$2.6 billion), and that over ₹1,000 crore (US$13 million) worth of streetwear is stolen every year.

The Sharks were impressed by Snitch clothing. They liked the product's unique selling proposition and its potential to solve a real problem. However, they were also concerned about the company's valuation. After some negotiation, Dungarwal and the Sharks reached an agreement. The Sharks invested ₹1.5 crore (US$200,000) in Snitch clothing in exchange for a 1% stake in the company. The Snitch clothing brand has continued to grow since its appearance on Shark Tank India. The company has expanded its product line and has begun to sell its products in international markets. Snitch clothing is now one of the leading brands in the fight against streetwear theft.
