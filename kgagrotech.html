KG Agrotech:  An Indian Startup Using Pesticide Spray Carts to Revolutionise Agriculture

KG Agrotech is an Indian firm that has created a one-of-a-kind pesticide spray cart that is assisting farmers in increasing their productivity and efficiency. Because the cart is lightweight and compact, it is easy to manoeuvre through tight agricultural paths. It can also spray up to 200 feet, allowing farmers to swiftly and easily cover a big area. Kamlesh Ghumare, a farmer from Nashik, Maharashtra, invented the pesticide spray cart. Kamlesh was having difficulty spraying pesticides on his crops because traditional methods were time-consuming and labor-intensive. He decided to create a more efficient solution, which resulted in the pesticide spray cart.

Source:sharktanktalks.com

KG Agrotech Shark Tank Story


In 2023, KG Agrotech appeared on the Indian business reality show Shark Tank India. Kamlesh Ghumare, a farmer from Nashik, Maharashtra, created the company. Kamlesh presented his pesticide spray cart to the show's investors, who were impressed.
The CEO of Lenskart, Peyush Bansal, was particularly interested in KG Agrotech. In exchange for a 40% ownership in the company, he gave 50 lakhs (US$66,000). Kamlesh accepted the offer, and KG Agrotech became India's first startup to land a transaction on Shark Tank.
Shark Tank India's funding has enabled KG Agrotech to expand its activities. The business has improved its manufacturing capacity and can now sell pesticide spray carts to farmers across India. In the future, KG Agrotech intends to export its products to other countries.
Kamlesh Ghumare is a self-made business owner who overcame numerous obstacles to bring his pesticide spray cart to market. He serves as an example to other farmers and businesses, demonstrating that anyone can achieve their goals if they are prepared to work hard and never give up.
