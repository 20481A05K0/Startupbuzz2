Urban Monkey:  The Indian Streetwear Brand That's Taking Over

In 2013, Yash Gangwal launched the streetwear company Urban Monkey in Mumbai, India. The company was founded in the beginning to give skateboarders, athletes, and underground artists in India the things they so much needed. However, Urban Monkey has since developed into one of the most well-known streetwear companies in the nation, with a vast selection of goods that appeal to many different consumer groups. Numerous elements contributed to Urban Monkey's success. First of all, the company has a thorough awareness of the streetwear market in India. They are able to deliver the goods that their clients require at a fair price because they are aware of what those goods are. Second, Urban Monkey is well-represented on social media. They use Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter, where they have a sizable following, to interact with their customers and market their business. Third, Gully Gang, Raftaar, and Rannvijay Singha are some of the top hip-hop artists from India with whom Urban Monkey has worked. These partnerships have enhanced the brand's visibility and drawn in new clients.

The Shark Tank Story


Urban Monkey made an appearance on Shark Tank India's inaugural season in 2022. The business was looking for a 50 lakh rupee (US$66,000) investment in exchange for 10% equity. They presented their company to the sharks, emphasising their strong brand, devoted clientele, and expanding internet sales. Urban Monkey won the sharks over, but they were also worried about the company's valuation. Aman Gupta of boAt made an offer that Yash and Nikhil ultimately chose to take out of all the deals that they had to give. Aman contributed 50 lakhs in exchange for 15% of Urban Monkey's ownership, valuing the company at 333 lakhs (US$440,000). Urban Monkey has expanded ever since it was featured on Shark Tank India. The business has boosted its internet sales, added new stores, and extended its product portfolio. One of the most popular streetwear brands in India right now, Urban Monkey is positioned for continued expansion in the years to come. .

Conclusion


A growing streetwear company is Urban Monkey. The company is able to establish a personal connection with its clients since it has a thorough understanding of the Indian market. Along with growing its product line, Urban Monkey is also launching new retail locations. The company is in a good position to grow during the coming years.
